Welcome to Euclid Point

Euclid Point is a community of engineers, software professionals, mathematicians, and students with a special interest in mathematics and computer science literature. Related areas like algorithms, cryptography, physics, logic puzzles, etc. are on-topic in this community. If you find these topics interesting, you are welcome to join us and be a part of our community. You are also welcome to invite your friends and colleagues and help this community grow.
